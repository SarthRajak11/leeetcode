LeetCode

LeetCode is a powerful online platform designed for coding interview preparation. It offers a wide range of algorithmic and data structure problems to help users strengthen their problem-solving skills.

The platform is popular among software engineers and coding enthusiasts, especially those preparing for technical interviews at top tech companies. It supports multiple programming languages like Java, Python, C++, and JavaScript.

LeetCode provides both free and premium subscription tiers. Premium users gain access to additional interview-specific problems and company-wise question lists. There's also support for mock interviews and coding contests.

Weekly and biweekly coding contests attract thousands of participants globally, with real-time rankings and performance analytics.
